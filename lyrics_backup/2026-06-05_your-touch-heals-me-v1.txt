[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Your fingertips tracing my skin in the midnight
Washing the dust right off my soul
Pulling me into the warmth of your sweet light
Your touch heals me and takes control

[Verse 1]
The fire is low but the embers are burning
Shadows are sliding across the floor
You stop the whole wild world from turning
And leave my body just begging for more

[Pre-Chorus]
Pull me closer to your breathing
Let the midnight slip away
Every secret I've been concealing
Fades to nothing when you stay

[Chorus]
Your touch heals me in the quiet darkness
Melting the winter right out of my bones
Lost in the fire of your gentle closeness
I am a drifter who finally came home

[Post-Chorus]
Take my edges, make them right
Hold me in this candlelight

[Verse 2]
I feel the rhythm of your slow heartbeat
Drawing me down to a deeper space
Tangled together in the midnight heat
I want to memorize every trace

[Pre-Chorus 2]

Let the midnight slip away
Every secret I've been concealing

[Chorus]

[Post-Chorus 2]
Take my edges, make them right
Hold me in this candlelight

[Bridge]
Every fence that I built out of sorrow
Every wall that I tried to defend
They fall away as I look to tomorrow
Knowing your love is the place where I mend

[Spoken]
Even a restless soul like Saeed can finally lay these burdens down
When your fingertips trace away the weight of this lonely town

[Final Chorus]
Your touch heals me in the quiet darkness
Melting the winter right out of my bones
Lost in the fire of your gentle closeness
I am a drifter who finally came home
